    About VSRA

    The Vancouver Seniors Resources Alliance connects seniors to services, programs, and supports across all Vancouver neighbourhoods.

    Our Mission

    We believe every senior in Vancouver should be able to find the support they need, close to home and easy to access. This directory is free, up-to-date, and organized to be as simple as possible to use.

    Whether you're looking for medical care, social programs, housing help, or just a place to connect with others — this is your starting point.

    Know of a missing resource?
    Please contact us and we'll add it to the directory.
